Chisato Nishikigi

Chisato Nishikigi (錦木 千束, Nishikigi Chisato) is the main protagonist of the original anime series, Lycoris Recoil. She is the partner of Takina Inoue.

Lycoris Recoil (リコリス・リコイル, Rikorisu Rikoiru) is an original Japanese anime series produced by A-1 Pictures. The anime series premiered from July 2 to September 24, 2022.
